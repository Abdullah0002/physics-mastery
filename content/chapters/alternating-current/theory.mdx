## 1. AC Voltage and Current

<Formula label="AC Voltage">
$$V = V_0\sin(\omega t), \quad I = I_0\sin(\omega t + \phi)$$
</Formula>

**RMS values:**

$$V_{rms} = \frac{V_0}{\sqrt{2}}, \quad I_{rms} = \frac{I_0}{\sqrt{2}}$$

---

## 2. AC Across Individual Elements

### Resistor

$V_R = IR$. Voltage and current **in phase**.

### Inductor

$$X_L = \omega L, \quad V_L = IX_L$$

Current **lags** voltage by $90°$.

### Capacitor

$$X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C}, \quad V_C = IX_C$$

Current **leads** voltage by $90°$.

<Callout type="tip">
Mnemonic: **CIVIL** — In a **C**apacitor, **I** leads **V**. In an Inductor (**L**), **V** leads **I**.
</Callout>

---

## 3. Series LCR Circuit

Impedance:

<Formula label="Impedance">
$$Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Phase angle">
$$\tan\phi = \frac{X_L - X_C}{R}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Current amplitude">
$$I_0 = \frac{V_0}{Z}$$
</Formula>

---

## 4. Phasor Diagram

- $V_R$ along current direction (in phase).
- $V_L$ leads current by $90°$.
- $V_C$ lags current by $90°$.
- Total voltage = vector sum.

---

## 5. Resonance in Series LCR

At resonance: $X_L = X_C$, so $Z = R$ (minimum).

<Formula label="Resonant Frequency">
$$\omega_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}, \quad f_0 = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}}$$
</Formula>

Current is maximum at resonance: $I_{max} = V_0/R$.

**Quality factor (Q):**

$$Q = \frac{\omega_0 L}{R} = \frac{1}{\omega_0 CR} = \frac{1}{R}\sqrt{\frac{L}{C}}$$

$Q$ measures sharpness of resonance. High $Q$ = narrow bandwidth.

---

## 6. Parallel LCR Circuit

At resonance: $X_L = X_C$, current through $L$ and $C$ cancel. Impedance is **maximum** (ideally infinite for ideal components).

---

## 7. Power in AC Circuits

<Formula label="Average Power">
$$P = V_{rms}I_{rms}\cos\phi$$
</Formula>

**Power factor:** $\cos\phi = R/Z$

| Circuit | $\phi$ | $\cos\phi$ | Power |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pure resistor | 0 | 1 | $V_{rms}I_{rms}$ |
| Pure inductor | $90°$ | 0 | 0 (wattless) |
| Pure capacitor | $-90°$ | 0 | 0 (wattless) |
| LCR at resonance | 0 | 1 | Maximum |

**Wattless current:** The reactive component of current that contributes no power.

---

## 8. Transformer (recap)

$$\frac{V_s}{V_p} = \frac{N_s}{N_p}, \quad P_p = P_s \text{ (ideal)}$$

---

## 9. AC Generator

A coil of $N$ turns, area $A$, rotating at $\omega$ in field $B$:

$$\mathcal{E} = NBA\omega\sin(\omega t) = \mathcal{E}_0\sin(\omega t)$$

$$\mathcal{E}_0 = NBA\omega$$

---

## 10. Summary

| Quantity | Formula |
|---|---|
| $X_L$ | $\omega L$ |
| $X_C$ | $1/(\omega C)$ |
| Impedance $Z$ | $\sqrt{R^2+(X_L-X_C)^2}$ |
| Resonant $\omega_0$ | $1/\sqrt{LC}$ |
| Power factor | $\cos\phi = R/Z$ |
| Average power | $V_{rms}I_{rms}\cos\phi$ |
| Quality factor | $\omega_0 L/R$ |
| $V_{rms}$ | $V_0/\sqrt{2}$ |
