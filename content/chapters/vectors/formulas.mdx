## Vector Representation

<Formula label="Vector in Component Form">
$$\vec{A} = A_x\,\hat{i} + A_y\,\hat{j} + A_z\,\hat{k}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Magnitude of a Vector">
$$|\vec{A}| = A = \sqrt{A_x^2 + A_y^2 + A_z^2}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Unit Vector">
$$\hat{A} = \frac{\vec{A}}{|\vec{A}|}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Direction Cosines">
$$\cos\alpha = \frac{A_x}{A},\quad \cos\beta = \frac{A_y}{A},\quad \cos\gamma = \frac{A_z}{A}$$
$$\cos^2\alpha + \cos^2\beta + \cos^2\gamma = 1$$
</Formula>

---

## Vector Addition & Subtraction

<Formula label="Triangle Law of Vector Addition">
$$\vec{R} = \vec{A} + \vec{B}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Parallelogram Law — Magnitude">
$$R = \sqrt{A^2 + B^2 + 2AB\cos\theta}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Parallelogram Law — Direction">
$$\tan\phi = \frac{B\sin\theta}{A + B\cos\theta}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Magnitude of Difference">
$$|\vec{A} - \vec{B}| = \sqrt{A^2 + B^2 - 2AB\cos\theta}$$
</Formula>

<Callout type="tip">
Maximum resultant = $A + B$ (when $\theta = 0°$); Minimum resultant = $|A - B|$ (when $\theta = 180°$).
</Callout>

## Resolution of Vectors

<Formula label="2D Resolution">
$$A_x = A\cos\theta,\quad A_y = A\sin\theta$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="3D Resolution along Axes">
$$\vec{A} = A\cos\alpha\,\hat{i} + A\cos\beta\,\hat{j} + A\cos\gamma\,\hat{k}$$
</Formula>

---

## Dot Product (Scalar Product)

<Formula label="Dot Product — Algebraic">
$$\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B} = AB\cos\theta$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Dot Product — Component Form">
$$\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B} = A_xB_x + A_yB_y + A_zB_z$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Standard Dot Products">
$$\hat{i}\cdot\hat{i} = \hat{j}\cdot\hat{j} = \hat{k}\cdot\hat{k} = 1;\quad \hat{i}\cdot\hat{j} = \hat{j}\cdot\hat{k} = \hat{k}\cdot\hat{i} = 0$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Angle Between Two Vectors">
$$\cos\theta = \frac{\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B}}{AB}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Projection of A on B">
$$\text{proj}_{\vec{B}}\vec{A} = \frac{\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B}}{B}$$
</Formula>

---

## Cross Product (Vector Product)

<Formula label="Cross Product — Magnitude">
$$|\vec{A}\times\vec{B}| = AB\sin\theta$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Cross Product — Determinant Form">
$$\vec{A}\times\vec{B} = \begin{vmatrix}\hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k}\\ A_x & A_y & A_z\\ B_x & B_y & B_z\end{vmatrix}$$
$$= \hat{i}(A_yB_z - A_zB_y) - \hat{j}(A_xB_z - A_zB_x) + \hat{k}(A_xB_y - A_yB_x)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Standard Cross Products">
$$\hat{i}\times\hat{j}=\hat{k},\quad \hat{j}\times\hat{k}=\hat{i},\quad \hat{k}\times\hat{i}=\hat{j}$$
$$\hat{i}\times\hat{i}=\hat{j}\times\hat{j}=\hat{k}\times\hat{k}=\vec{0}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Anti-commutativity">
$$\vec{A}\times\vec{B} = -\vec{B}\times\vec{A}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Area of Parallelogram">
$$\text{Area} = |\vec{A}\times\vec{B}|$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Area of Triangle">
$$\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}|\vec{A}\times\vec{B}|$$
</Formula>

---

## Scalar Triple Product

<Formula label="Scalar Triple Product">
$$\vec{A}\cdot(\vec{B}\times\vec{C}) = \begin{vmatrix}A_x & A_y & A_z\\ B_x & B_y & B_z\\ C_x & C_y & C_z\end{vmatrix}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Volume of Parallelepiped">
$$V = |\vec{A}\cdot(\vec{B}\times\vec{C})|$$
</Formula>

<Callout type="tip">
Cyclic permutation leaves the scalar triple product unchanged: $\vec{A}\cdot(\vec{B}\times\vec{C}) = \vec{B}\cdot(\vec{C}\times\vec{A}) = \vec{C}\cdot(\vec{A}\times\vec{B})$.
</Callout>

---

## Differentiation of Vectors

<Formula label="Derivative of a Vector">
$$\frac{d\vec{A}}{dt} = \frac{dA_x}{dt}\hat{i} + \frac{dA_y}{dt}\hat{j} + \frac{dA_z}{dt}\hat{k}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Derivative of Dot Product">
$$\frac{d}{dt}(\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B}) = \dot{\vec{A}}\cdot\vec{B} + \vec{A}\cdot\dot{\vec{B}}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Derivative of Cross Product">
$$\frac{d}{dt}(\vec{A}\times\vec{B}) = \dot{\vec{A}}\times\vec{B} + \vec{A}\times\dot{\vec{B}}$$
</Formula>

---

## Special Vector Identities

<Formula label="BAC-CAB Rule">
$$\vec{A}\times(\vec{B}\times\vec{C}) = \vec{B}(\vec{A}\cdot\vec{C}) - \vec{C}(\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B})$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Condition for Parallel Vectors">
$$\vec{A}\times\vec{B} = \vec{0} \iff \vec{A} \parallel \vec{B}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Condition for Perpendicular Vectors">
$$\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B} = 0 \iff \vec{A} \perp \vec{B}$$
</Formula>
