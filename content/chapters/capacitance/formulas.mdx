## 1. Core Definitions

<Formula label="Capacitance">
$$C=\frac{Q}{V}\qquad [\text{F}=\text{C/V}],\qquad [C]=\mathrm{M^{-1}L^{-2}T^{4}I^{2}}$$
</Formula>

- $C$ depends **only** on geometry and medium ﻗ°½ never on $Q$ or $V$.
- $\varepsilon_0=8.85\times10^{-12}\,\text{F/m}$, $\ \dfrac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}=9\times10^{9}$.

---

## 2. Capacitance of Standard Geometries

| Geometry | Capacitance |
|---|---|
| Isolated sphere (radius $R$) | $C=4\pi\varepsilon_0 R$ |
| Concentric spheres ($a<b$) | $C=4\pi\varepsilon_0\dfrac{ab}{b-a}$ |
| Parallel plate (vacuum) | $C_0=\dfrac{\varepsilon_0 A}{d}$ |
| Parallel plate (dielectric $K$) | $C=\dfrac{K\varepsilon_0 A}{d}=KC_0$ |
| Cylindrical (coaxial, length $L$) | $C=\dfrac{2\pi\varepsilon_0 L}{\ln(b/a)}$ |

---

## 3. Field, Potential, Charge (Parallel Plate)

$$\sigma=\frac{Q}{A},\qquad E=\frac{\sigma}{\varepsilon_0}=\frac{V}{d},\qquad V=Ed,\qquad E_{\text{with }K}=\frac{E_0}{K}.$$

---

## 4. Combinations

<Formula label="Series and Parallel">
$$\text{Series: }\ \frac{1}{C_{eq}}=\sum\frac{1}{C_i}\quad(\text{same }Q);\qquad \text{Parallel: }\ C_{eq}=\sum C_i\quad(\text{same }V)$$
</Formula>

- Two in series: $C_{eq}=\dfrac{C_1C_2}{C_1+C_2}$, $\ \dfrac{V_1}{V_2}=\dfrac{C_2}{C_1}$.
- Series $C_{eq}<$ smallest; parallel $C_{eq}>$ largest.
- Charge in parallel: $Q_i=Q\dfrac{C_i}{\sum C}$.

---

## 5. Energy

<Formula label="Energy Stored and Density">
$$U=\frac{1}{2}QV=\frac{1}{2}CV^2=\frac{Q^2}{2C},\qquad u=\frac{1}{2}\varepsilon_0 E^2=\frac12 K\varepsilon_0E^2\ (\text{dielectric})$$
</Formula>

- Fixed $Q$ ﻗ│φ use $Q^2/2C$. Fixed $V$ ﻗ│φ use $\tfrac12CV^2$.
- Battery does $QV$; cap stores $\tfrac12QV$; **50٪ lost** when charging from empty.

---

## 6. Dielectrics & Polarization

$$K=\frac{C}{C_0}=\frac{E_0}{E}=\frac{\varepsilon}{\varepsilon_0},\quad \chi_e=K-1,\quad P=\varepsilon_0(K-1)E,\quad \sigma_b=\sigma_f\!\left(1-\frac1K\right),\quad D=\varepsilon_0KE.$$

---

## 7. Partial / Multi-Dielectric

| Configuration | Capacitance |
|---|---|
| Slab thickness $t<d$ (along field) | $C=\dfrac{\varepsilon_0 A}{d-t(1-1/K)}$ |
| Metal slab thickness $t$ | $C=\dfrac{\varepsilon_0 A}{d-t}$ |
| Stacked slabs (series) | $C=\dfrac{\varepsilon_0 A}{\sum d_i/K_i}$ |
| Side-by-side (parallel) | $C=\dfrac{\varepsilon_0}{d}\sum K_iA_i$ |
| Stacked $D$-continuity | $K_1E_1=K_2E_2$ |

---

## 8. Forces

$$F_{\text{plates}}=\frac{Q^2}{2\varepsilon_0 A}=\frac{\sigma^2A}{2\varepsilon_0},\qquad P=\frac{\sigma^2}{2\varepsilon_0}=\frac12\varepsilon_0E^2=u,$$
$$F_{\text{on dielectric}}=\frac{\varepsilon_0 b(K-1)V^2}{2d}\quad(\text{inward; energy method }F=\pm\tfrac{dU}{dx}).$$

---

## 9. Charge Sharing & RC

$$V_{common}=\frac{C_1V_1+C_2V_2}{C_1+C_2},\qquad \Delta U_{lost}=\frac{C_1C_2(V_1-V_2)^2}{2(C_1+C_2)}\ge0.$$
$$q_{\text{charge}}=Q_0(1-e^{-t/RC}),\quad q_{\text{discharge}}=Q_0e^{-t/RC},\quad \tau=RC.$$

---

## 10. ﻗﺝβ The Master Table ﻗ°½ Dielectric Insertion

| Quantity | Battery ON ($V$ fixed) | Battery OFF ($Q$ fixed) |
|---|---|---|
| $C$ | ﺃ«$K$ | ﺃ«$K$ |
| $Q$ | ﺃ«$K$ | unchanged |
| $V$ | unchanged | ﺃ٧$K$ |
| $E$ | unchanged | ﺃ٧$K$ |
| $U$ | ﺃ«$K$ (rises) | ﺃ٧$K$ (falls) |

<Callout type="important">
**First question, every time:** *Is the battery connected ($V$ fixed) or removed ($Q$ fixed)?* This table then gives every answer.
</Callout>

## 12. Last-Minute Tips

<Callout type="tip">
1. **Redraw every network** and label nodes before computing ﻗ°½ most errors are identification, not algebra.
2. **State what's fixed** ($Q$ or $V$) at the top of every dielectric/geometry-change problem.
3. **Pick the right energy form:** $Q^2/2C$ (fixed $Q$) or $\tfrac12CV^2$ (fixed $V$).
4. **Smaller series cap takes the larger voltage** ﻗ°½ and may break down first.
5. **Charge sharing always loses energy**; use the $\Delta U$ formula directly.
6. **DC steady state:** capacitor = open circuit; solve the resistor network, then $Q=CV$.
7. **Force uses $\sigma/2\varepsilon_0$** (other plate), not the full field.
8. **Limiting-case check** every derived formula ($K\to1$, $t\to0$, $b\to\infty$).
9. Units: convert ﺧﺱF/nF/pF ﻗ│φ F **before** squaring in energy formulas.
10. **Balanced bridge?** The bridge capacitor carries zero charge ﻗ°½ ignore it.
</Callout>

<Callout type="important">
**The seven non-negotiables:** $U=\tfrac12QV$ ﺁ٧ field between plates $=\sigma/\varepsilon_0$ but force uses $\sigma/2\varepsilon_0$ ﺁ٧ series same-$Q$ smaller-cap-bigger-$V$ ﺁ٧ dielectric raises $U$ only with battery on ﺁ٧ charge sharing always loses energy ﺁ٧ steady-state cap current is zero ﺁ٧ **always ask: $Q$ fixed or $V$ fixed?**
</Callout>
