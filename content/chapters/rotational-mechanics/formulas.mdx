## Moment of Inertia

<Formula label="Definition of Moment of Inertia">
$$I = \sum m_i r_i^2 = \int r^2\,dm$$
</Formula>

| Body | Axis | I |
|------|------|---|
| Thin rod (length L, mass M) | Through center, ⊥ | $\frac{1}{12}ML^2$ |
| Thin rod (length L, mass M) | Through end, ⊥ | $\frac{1}{3}ML^2$ |
| Solid cylinder / disc (radius R) | Symmetry axis | $\frac{1}{2}MR^2$ |
| Solid cylinder (radius R) | Diameter | $\frac{1}{4}MR^2 + \frac{1}{12}ML^2$ |
| Hollow cylinder / ring (radius R) | Symmetry axis | $MR^2$ |
| Hollow cylinder (radius R) | Diameter | $\frac{1}{2}MR^2$ |
| Solid sphere (radius R) | Diameter | $\frac{2}{5}MR^2$ |
| Hollow sphere (radius R) | Diameter | $\frac{2}{3}MR^2$ |
| Rectangular plate (a × b) | Through center, ⊥ | $\frac{M(a^2+b^2)}{12}$ |
| Annular disc (R₁, R₂) | Symmetry axis | $\frac{M(R_1^2+R_2^2)}{2}$ |

## Parallel Axis Theorem

<Formula label="Parallel Axis Theorem">
$$I = I_{\text{cm}} + Md^2$$
$$\text{(d = distance between parallel axes)}$$
</Formula>

---

## Perpendicular Axis Theorem

<Formula label="Perpendicular Axis Theorem (planar bodies only)">
$$I_z = I_x + I_y$$
$$\text{(x, y in plane; z perpendicular to plane)}$$
</Formula>

<Callout type="important">
Perpendicular axis theorem applies ONLY to planar (2D) bodies.
</Callout>

## Torque

<Formula label="Torque">
$$\vec{\tau} = \vec{r}\times\vec{F},\quad |\tau| = rF\sin\theta = Fd_\perp$$
$$\text{(where } d_\perp \text{ is perpendicular distance from axis)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Newton's Second Law for Rotation">
$$\tau_{\text{net}} = I\alpha$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Work Done by Torque">
$$W = \int \tau\,d\theta = \tau\,\theta \quad \text{(constant } \tau\text{)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Power of Torque">
$$P = \tau\omega$$
</Formula>

---

## Angular Momentum

<Formula label="Angular Momentum of Particle">
$$\vec{L} = \vec{r}\times\vec{p} = m\vec{r}\times\vec{v}$$
$$|\vec{L}| = mvr\sin\theta$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Angular Momentum of Rigid Body">
$$L = I\omega$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Newton's Second Law for Rotation (angular form)">
$$\tau_{\text{net}} = \frac{d\vec{L}}{dt}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Conservation of Angular Momentum">
$$\tau_{\text{ext}} = 0 \implies L = I\omega = \text{constant}$$
$$I_1\omega_1 = I_2\omega_2$$
</Formula>

---

## Rotational Kinetic Energy

<Formula label="Rotational KE">
$$KE_{\text{rot}} = \frac{1}{2}I\omega^2 = \frac{L^2}{2I}$$
</Formula>

---

## Rolling Without Slipping

<Formula label="Rolling Constraint">
$$v_{\text{cm}} = R\omega, \quad a_{\text{cm}} = R\alpha$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Total KE of Rolling Body">
$$KE_{\text{total}} = \frac{1}{2}mv_{\text{cm}}^2 + \frac{1}{2}I_{\text{cm}}\omega^2 = \frac{1}{2}mv_{\text{cm}}^2\left(1 + \frac{k^2}{R^2}\right)$$
$$\text{where } k^2 = I_{\text{cm}}/M \text{ (radius of gyration squared)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Acceleration of Rolling Body on Incline">
$$a = \frac{g\sin\theta}{1 + I_{\text{cm}}/(MR^2)} = \frac{g\sin\theta}{1 + k^2/R^2}$$
</Formula>

| Rolling Body | $k^2/R^2$ | Acceleration |
|---|---|---|
| Ring / hollow cylinder | 1 | $g\sin\theta/2$ |
| Solid cylinder / disc | 1/2 | $2g\sin\theta/3$ |
| Hollow sphere | 2/3 | $3g\sin\theta/5$ |
| Solid sphere | 2/5 | $5g\sin\theta/7$ |

<Formula label="Velocity at Bottom of Incline (Rolling)">
$$v = \sqrt{\frac{2gh}{1 + k^2/R^2}}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Friction Force for Rolling on Incline">
$$f = \frac{Mg\sin\theta}{1 + MR^2/I_{\text{cm}}} = \frac{Mg\sin\theta \cdot (k^2/R^2)}{1 + k^2/R^2}$$
</Formula>

---

## Velocity of Points on Rolling Body

<Formula label="Velocity of contact point">
$$v_{\text{contact}} = 0$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Velocity of top point">
$$v_{\text{top}} = 2v_{\text{cm}}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Velocity at angle θ from bottom">
$$v = 2v_{\text{cm}}\sin(\theta/2)$$
</Formula>

---

## Equilibrium of Rigid Bodies

<Formula label="Conditions for Equilibrium">
$$\sum \vec{F} = 0 \quad \text{(translational equilibrium)}$$
$$\sum \vec{\tau} = 0 \quad \text{(rotational equilibrium)}$$
</Formula>
