## Center of Mass — Discrete System

<Formula label="CoM Position (x-component)">
$$x_{\text{cm}} = \frac{\sum m_i x_i}{\sum m_i} = \frac{m_1 x_1 + m_2 x_2 + \cdots}{m_1 + m_2 + \cdots}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="CoM Position (3D)">
$$\vec{r}_{\text{cm}} = \frac{\sum m_i \vec{r}_i}{M}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Velocity of Center of Mass">
$$\vec{v}_{\text{cm}} = \frac{\sum m_i \vec{v}_i}{M} = \frac{\vec{p}_{\text{total}}}{M}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Acceleration of Center of Mass">
$$\vec{a}_{\text{cm}} = \frac{\vec{F}_{\text{ext}}}{M}$$
</Formula>

---

## Center of Mass — Continuous Bodies

<Formula label="CoM of Continuous Body">
$$\vec{r}_{\text{cm}} = \frac{\int \vec{r}\,dm}{\int dm}$$
</Formula>

| Shape | CoM Location |
|-------|-------------|
| Uniform rod (length L) | L/2 from one end |
| Semicircular arc (radius R) | $2R/\pi$ from center |
| Semicircular disc (radius R) | $4R/(3\pi)$ from center |
| Solid hemisphere (radius R) | $3R/8$ from flat face |
| Hollow hemisphere (radius R) | $R/2$ from flat face |
| Solid cone (height h) | $h/4$ from base |
| Hollow cone (height h) | $h/3$ from base |
| Triangle (height h) | $h/3$ from base |

## CoM of Remaining Body

<Formula label="CoM after Removing Part">
$$x_{\text{cm,remaining}} = \frac{M x_{\text{cm,whole}} - m\,x_{\text{cm,removed}}}{M - m}$$
</Formula>

---

## Motion of CoM and Newton's Laws

<Formula label="Internal Forces Cancel">
$$\vec{F}_{\text{int}} = 0 \implies \vec{F}_{\text{ext}} = M\vec{a}_{\text{cm}}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="CoM of Isolated System">
$$\vec{F}_{\text{ext}} = 0 \implies \vec{v}_{\text{cm}} = \text{const} \implies \vec{r}_{\text{cm}} = \text{const (if initially at rest)}$$
</Formula>

<Callout type="important">
In an explosion or internal interaction, the CoM continues on the same path with no change in velocity — only the external force changes CoM motion.
</Callout>

## Impulse and Momentum

<Formula label="Impulse-Momentum Theorem">
$$\vec{J} = \int \vec{F}\,dt = \Delta\vec{p} = m(\vec{v}_f - \vec{v}_i)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Conservation of Linear Momentum">
$$\vec{F}_{\text{ext}} = 0 \implies \vec{p}_{\text{total}} = \text{constant}$$
$$m_1\vec{v}_1 + m_2\vec{v}_2 = m_1\vec{u}_1 + m_2\vec{u}_2$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Recoil Velocity (Gun-Bullet)">
$$M V + m v = 0 \implies V = -\frac{mv}{M}$$
</Formula>

---

## Rocket Equation (Tsiolkovsky)

<Formula label="Rocket Thrust">
$$F_{\text{thrust}} = v_r \frac{dm}{dt}$$
$$\text{(where } v_r \text{ = exhaust speed relative to rocket, } dm/dt \text{ = fuel burn rate)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Rocket Equation (no gravity)">
$$v - u = v_r \ln\!\left(\frac{m_0}{m}\right)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Rocket Equation (with gravity, vertical)">
$$v = u + v_r \ln\!\left(\frac{m_0}{m}\right) - gt$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Burn Time and Mass">
$$m = m_0 - \dot{m}\,t \quad \text{(where } \dot{m} \text{ is mass flow rate)}$$
</Formula>

## Kinetic Energy in CoM Frame

<Formula label="KE in Lab Frame vs CoM Frame">
$$KE_{\text{lab}} = KE_{\text{cm}} + \frac{1}{2}Mv_{\text{cm}}^2$$
$$KE_{\text{cm}} = \frac{1}{2}\mu\,v_{\text{rel}}^2, \quad \mu = \frac{m_1 m_2}{m_1+m_2}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Reduced Mass">
$$\mu = \frac{m_1 m_2}{m_1 + m_2}$$
</Formula>

## 2D Collision

<Formula label="2D Elastic Collision Angle (equal masses, one at rest)">
$$\theta_1 + \theta_2 = 90°$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Oblique Elastic Collision — Momentum Components">
$$m_1 u_1 = m_1 v_1\cos\theta_1 + m_2 v_2\cos\theta_2$$
$$0 = m_1 v_1\sin\theta_1 - m_2 v_2\sin\theta_2$$
</Formula>
