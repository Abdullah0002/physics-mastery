## Thermal Expansion

<Formula label="Linear Expansion">
$$\Delta L = L_0\,\alpha\,\Delta T, \quad L = L_0(1 + \alpha\,\Delta T)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Area Expansion">
$$\Delta A = A_0\,\beta\,\Delta T, \quad \beta = 2\alpha$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Volume Expansion">
$$\Delta V = V_0\,\gamma\,\Delta T, \quad \gamma = 3\alpha$$
$$\beta = 2\alpha,\quad \gamma = 3\alpha$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Anomalous Expansion of Water">
$$\text{Water has maximum density at } 4°\text{C}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Thermal Stress">
$$\sigma = Y\alpha\,\Delta T \quad \text{(rod clamped at both ends)}$$
$$F = YA\alpha\,\Delta T$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Apparent Expansion of Liquid in Container">
$$\gamma_{\text{apparent}} = \gamma_{\text{liquid}} - \gamma_{\text{container}}$$
$$\gamma_{\text{container}} = 3\alpha_{\text{container}}$$
</Formula>

## Calorimetry

<Formula label="Heat Absorbed / Released">
$$Q = mc\,\Delta T$$
$$\text{(m = mass, c = specific heat capacity)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Molar Heat Capacity">
$$Q = nC\,\Delta T, \quad C = Mc_{\text{specific}} \quad (\text{M = molar mass})$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Latent Heat">
$$Q = mL \quad \text{(no temperature change during phase transition)}$$
$$L_f \approx 3.36\times10^5\ \text{J kg}^{-1}\ \text{(fusion/ice)},\quad L_v \approx 2.26\times10^6\ \text{J kg}^{-1}\ \text{(vaporisation/water)}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Heat Capacity">
$$C_{\text{body}} = mc = \frac{Q}{\Delta T}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Water Equivalent">
$$W = \frac{mc}{c_w} \quad (c_w = 4200\ \text{J kg}^{-1}\text{K}^{-1})$$
</Formula>

## Heat Transfer — Conduction

<Formula label="Fourier's Law of Heat Conduction">
$$\frac{dQ}{dt} = -KA\frac{dT}{dx}$$
$$H = \frac{KA\,\Delta T}{l}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Thermal Resistance">
$$R_{\text{th}} = \frac{l}{KA}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Series Combination of Slabs">
$$R_{\text{total}} = R_1 + R_2 + \cdots, \quad H = \frac{\Delta T_{\text{total}}}{R_{\text{total}}}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Parallel Combination of Slabs">
$$\frac{1}{R_{\text{total}}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \cdots, \quad H_{\text{total}} = H_1 + H_2$$
</Formula>

## Heat Transfer — Radiation

<Formula label="Stefan-Boltzmann Law">
$$P = \sigma A T^4, \quad \sigma = 5.67\times10^{-8}\ \text{W m}^{-2}\text{K}^{-4}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Emissive Power of a Body">
$$E = e\sigma T^4 \quad (e = \text{emissivity}, 0 \leq e \leq 1)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Net Radiation Power">
$$P_{\text{net}} = e\sigma A(T^4 - T_0^4)$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Wien's Displacement Law">
$$\lambda_m T = b, \quad b = 2.898\times10^{-3}\ \text{m K}$$
$$\lambda_m = \frac{b}{T}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Newton's Law of Cooling">
$$\frac{dT}{dt} = -k(T - T_0)$$
$$T(t) = T_0 + (T_i - T_0)e^{-kt}$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Newton's Cooling — Average Form (approximate)">
$$\frac{T_1 - T_2}{t} = k\left(\frac{T_1 + T_2}{2} - T_0\right)$$
</Formula>

---

## Kirchhoff's Law of Radiation

<Formula label="Kirchhoff's Law">
$$\frac{E_\lambda}{a_\lambda} = E_{b,\lambda}(T) \quad \text{(emissive power / absorptivity = blackbody emissive power)}$$
$$\text{Good emitters are good absorbers (and vice versa)}$$
</Formula>

## Temperature Scales

<Formula label="Celsius to Kelvin">
$$T(K) = T(°C) + 273.15$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="Fahrenheit to Celsius">
$$T(°C) = \frac{5}{9}[T(°F) - 32]$$
</Formula>

<Formula label="General Conversion">
$$\frac{C - 0}{100} = \frac{F - 32}{180} = \frac{K - 273}{100}$$
</Formula>
